LIONS

Lions are large carnivorous mammals belonging to the genus Panthera and the family Felidae. They are known for their distinctive manes, with manes being more prominent in males, and their powerful build.

Lions are native to Africa and a small population exists in the Gir Forest National Park in India. They primarily inhabit grasslands and savannas, forming social groups known as prides.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the lions in the African Savanna exhibit, basking in the sun near the rocky outcrop.
