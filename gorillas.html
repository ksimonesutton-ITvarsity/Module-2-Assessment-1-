GORILLAS

Gorillas are large primates belonging to the family Hominidae. They are divided into two species: the eastern gorilla (Gorilla beringei) and the western gorilla (Gorilla gorilla). Gorillas are known for their robust build, dark fur, and imposing presence.

They inhabit the forests of Central Africa and are primarily herbivores, consuming leaves, shoots, fruits, and occasionally insects. Gorillas live in groups called troops, led by a dominant male known as a silverback.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gorillas in the Primate Enclosure, nestled within the lush jungle habitat.
