PANDAS

Pandas are large, black-and-white bears native to China. They belong to the bear family, Ursidae, and are classified into two species: the giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) and the red panda (Ailurus fulgens).

Giant pandas primarily inhabit bamboo forests in the mountain ranges of central China, while red pandas are found in the forests of the Himalayas and southwestern China. Pandas are primarily herbivores, with bamboo making up the majority of their diet.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the pandas in the Bamboo Grove exhibit, munching on bamboo shoots and playing in their forested habitat.
